# 00 — Introduction

## Who am I?

I'm Kaliswary Ramish, a Senior Fraud Operations & Financial Crime professional based in Petaling Jaya, Malaysia, with 9+ years of experience in fintech payment operations and fraud operations. Most of that experience has been built at Grab (GPay Networks Sdn. Bhd.), where I've moved from front-line payment support through to leading first-line financial crime operations for a 12-member regional team spanning five Southeast Asian markets.

## What do I specialize in?

My day-to-day work sits at the intersection of fraud investigation, merchant risk, chargebacks, and account takeover investigation — supported by transaction monitoring, AML/KYC processes, and law enforcement collaboration. I also design and build the tooling that makes that work scalable: SQL-driven analytics, Tableau dashboards, Microsoft Dynamics 365 workflows, Google Apps Script automation, and AI-assisted investigation tooling.

I was recognized with the **SPF Outstanding Stakeholder Award (2025)** from the Singapore Police Force for sustained partnership on fraud prevention, scam tracing, and law enforcement escalation controls.

## Why did I build this repository?

This repository, **Fraud-Operations-Lab**, is a portfolio documenting the frameworks, thinking, and tooling behind my fraud operations and financial crime work. It's meant to give recruiters, hiring managers, and collaborators an honest, structured view of what I actually do — not a marketing pitch.

## What can recruiters expect?

Every claim in this repository is grounded in real work experience. Where something isn't yet documented or confirmed, it's marked as:

> `TODO: Confirm with portfolio owner`

rather than filled in with assumptions. Nothing here is exaggerated or invented.
