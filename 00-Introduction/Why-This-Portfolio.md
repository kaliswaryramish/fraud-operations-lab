# 💡 Why This Portfolio

Most of my work over the past 9+ years has happened inside internal systems: case management tools, internal dashboards, Dynamics 365 workflows, SOPs shared only within a regional team. It's real, high-impact work, but by nature it isn't visible outside the organization it was built in.

This repository, **Fraud-Operations-Lab**, exists to make that work legible to people outside Grab: recruiters, hiring managers, and peers in fraud operations and financial crime compliance who want to understand how I think and what I've actually built — not just a list of job titles.

I'm building this now because I'm exploring **Next level fraud risk and financial crime compliance roles** beyond my current market, and a static resume doesn't have room to show the reasoning behind the work — only the outcomes. This portfolio is meant to fill that gap honestly, without overstating what's here.
