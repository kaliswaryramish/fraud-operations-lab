# Career Journey

My career has been built entirely within Grab (GPay Networks Sdn. Bhd.), progressing from front-line payment operations into senior fraud and financial crime leadership across five Southeast Asian markets.

## Payment Support Executive
**April 2017 – October 2018**

Started in regional GrabPay payment operations and trust & safety, handling KYC screening, transaction monitoring, and compliance-aligned escalation resolution. This is where the foundation for everything after it was built — understanding the transaction lifecycle from the ground up.

## Risk Analyst
**October 2018 – December 2021**

Moved into dedicated risk casework: resolving complex cases across payments, banking, and chargebacks. Built the first SQL scripts and Tableau dashboards used for exposure analysis and ongoing monitoring, and reduced case handling time by 50% through process optimization and improved risk assessment. Also began training other analysts on SQL and SOPs and advising Policy on investigation framework enhancements.

## Risk Operations Specialist
**January 2022 – March 2024**

Took on end-to-end law enforcement workflow design — building escalation controls for law enforcement cases and establishing structured, time-bound resolution plans for senior stakeholder escalations. Built out executive dashboards and MI using Tableau, Zendesk, and SQL, and led quality assurance of control framework SOPs before publication.

## Risk Operations Senior Specialist
**April 2024 – Present**

Currently leading first-line financial crime operations for a 12-member regional team across five Southeast Asian markets, with responsibility for risk-based decision-making, quality assurance, and SLA performance. Key work in this role includes:

- Acting as the primary liaison with the Singapore Police Force on financial crime prevention and law enforcement escalation, recognized with the **SPF Outstanding Stakeholder Award (2025)**
- Owning the design and implementation of an automated investigation workflow for law enforcement tooling, reducing per-case handling time from 30 to 10 minutes and saving roughly 33 operational hours monthly across ~100 cases
- Partnering with Product, Data Analytics, and Engineering on an AI-powered investigation platform that standardized decisioning and improved investigation efficiency without added headcount
- Redesigning Microsoft Dynamics 365 workflows into a single source of truth, removing duplicate processes and enabling real-time MI
- Automating operational reporting with Google Apps Script for real-time management information
- Launching an AI-powered knowledge platform centralizing investigation SOPs
- Representing Grab in National Fraud Portal and regulatory reporting initiatives
- Partnering with Policy and business leadership on merchant risk assessment and emerging financial crime typologies via SQL analytics
