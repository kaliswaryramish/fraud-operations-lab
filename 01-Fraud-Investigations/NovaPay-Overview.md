> these investigations a consistent, confidentiality-safe backdrop.

## Company Snapshot

- **Name:** NovaPay
- **Sector:** Digital payments / fintech
- **Region:** Southeast Asia (multi-market)
- **Scale:** Millions of registered users, tens of thousands of merchants

## Products

| Product | Description |
|---|---|
| Digital Wallet | Stored-value e-wallet for P2P and merchant payments |
| QR Payments | Scan-to-pay at physical merchant locations |
| Merchant Payments | Payment acceptance tooling for online and offline merchants |
| Online Card Payments | Card-on-file and checkout payments |
| P2P Transfers | User-to-user money transfers |
| Bill Payments | Utility and service bill payments through the wallet |

## Why this matters for fraud

Each product line carries a different fraud profile — QR payments are exposed to merchant collusion and
counterfeit QR codes, P2P transfers are exposed to scam-induced transfers and mule accounts, and card
payments are exposed to chargeback fraud. NovaPay's fraud operations team investigates across all of them.
