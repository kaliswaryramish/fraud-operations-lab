# Interview Guide

**Purpose:** How to use this repo when discussing it in an interview.
**Estimated reading time:** 3 minutes
⬅️ [Back to README](./README.md)

## How to talk about this repo

- Lead with the **business problem**, not the SQL. Interviewers remember judgment, not queries.
- If asked "walk me through one investigation," pick the one most relevant to the role you're interviewing
  for (chargebacks → payments risk roles; ATO → trust & safety roles; trend analysis → strategy/leadership
  roles).
- Be ready to explain **why** a recommendation was practical, not just what it was.
- Each investigation folder has its own `Interview-Questions.md` and `Interview-Answers.md` — these are
  the specific talking points for that case.

## Good framing sentences

- "The business problem was X, so I started by confirming whether the pattern was real before writing any
  SQL."
- "The SQL confirmed the hypothesis, but the harder part was deciding what the business could realistically
  do about it."
- "The recommendation had to work within existing operational capacity — not just be theoretically correct."
